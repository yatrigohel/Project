Frequently Asked Questions

What types of electronics do you sell?

+

We offer a wide range of electronics including TVs, mobile phones, refrigerators, washing machines, audio systems, and more!

Do you offer home delivery?

+

Yes, we provide free home delivery within Ahmedabad city limits for orders above ₹5,000.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and EMI options via select banks.

Is there a warranty on the products?

+

All products come with a manufacturer’s warranty. We also offer extended warranty plans on selected items.

Do you offer repair services?

+

Yes, we have authorized service support and repair tie-ups for most brands. Visit our store or call us for details.

Can I return or exchange a product?

+

Returns and exchanges are accepted within 7 days of purchase with a valid receipt, only for unopened and undamaged items.
